Induction Motors

There are several kinds of induction motors. Induction motors are divided according to the types of rotors. The types of induction machine under this classification are:

  1. Squirrel-cage rotor induction motors
  2. Wound-rotor induction motors

There are several advantages of induction motors:

  • Induction motors are simple and rugged in construction
  • Induction motors are cheaper in cost due to the absence of brushes
